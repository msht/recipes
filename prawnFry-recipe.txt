Ingredients:
Cleaned Prawn - 20 small pieces
Grated Coconut - 1 tbsp
Big Onion - 1 chopped finely
Oil - 1 tbsp

For the marination:
Ginger Garlic Paste - 2 heaped tsp
Turmeric Powder - 1/2 tsp
Red Chilli Powder - 1 tsp
Coriander Powder - 1.5 tsp
Garam Masala Powder - 1 tsp
Lemon Juice - 1/4 of a lemon
Salt - to taste

To temper:
Fennel Seeds - 1 tsp
Curry Leaves - a small sprig
Oil - 2 tsp

Directions:
  1. Devein the prawns as mentioned in the link in the ingredients list.
  Rinse it well and transfer it to a mixin bowl.
  Add all the ingredients listed under 'to marinade' mix well.
  Marinade for atleast 30mins.
  2. Heat oil in a pan add the masala coated prawns and saute for 2mins.
  Then add little water.
  3. Cook covered for few mins till prawn gets cooked until soft. Keep aside.
  4. Heat oil - 
  add the items listed under 'to temper' then add small onion and fry till slightly browned.
  Then add cooked prawns.
  5. Saute for a minute.Check and adjust salt at this stage.
  Then add coconut and give a quick stir.
